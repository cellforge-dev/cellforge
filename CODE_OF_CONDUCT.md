# Code Of Conduct

CellForge uses a simple standard: be direct, respectful, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Discuss code, design, and technical tradeoffs clearly.
- Assume maintainers and contributors are working in good faith.
- Keep issues and pull requests actionable.
- Give enough context for others to reproduce bugs or evaluate suggestions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Spam, low-effort promotion, or unrelated issue traffic.
- Publishing private information.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions after maintainers ask to refocus.

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments and issues that do not follow this standard.
